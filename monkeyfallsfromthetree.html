The Wonderful Monkey/ Short story

In one enchanting fable, there was once a monkey named Monkey. One day Monkey thought of stealing bananas, but Crow the spider stopped them. "You imbecile monkey, how dare you steal bananas". Shock spread over the monkey's face, then the crow pushed to monkey to the ground

Then the crow said, "the monkey does not fall far from the tree"

This is a good lesson for you, the monkey doesn't fall far from the tree
